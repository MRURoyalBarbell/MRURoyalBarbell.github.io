Royal Barbell

Royal Barbell is a Mount Royal University club that emphasizes the importance of fitness and community. We welcome a wide variety of disciplines that include, but are not limited to, bodybuilding, weightlifting, power lifting, circuit training, etc. There is no set requirement for skill level; beginners and advanced athletes are welcome!

Join us!

Join the club!

You can visit our official SAMRU club page here

For more information send us an email at RoyalBarbell@gmail.com or find us at Facebook.


Joining our club offers various benefits. For a mere $10, you instantly enjoy discounts off all appeal and on most major seminars. You also gain priority seats for events, mock meets, and seminars. We also host a meeting at the end of every month where you can meet like minded individuals, and served with macro-friendly food. Another benefit of being a Royal Barbell member is that you will have the opportunity to be apart of a group discount for Canada's #1 powerlifting and strength supplier, InnerStrength Products! You also get the nice warm fuzzy feeling inside that you helped support our club and MRU spirit!


You can join us even if you’re from another school. Although only MRU students can run for executives, there are no restriction on who can join our club so bring your friends.


We look forward to seeing you and wish you luck in your athletic endeavors.

Merry Christmas 2016!

Seasons Greeting #RoyalBarbell Fam! We are so excited to announce that for the month of December we will be having our 12 days of Fitmas contest! We know that you are going to be busy this month, but we want to encourage you all to stay active through the holidays! To participate in the contest: 1. You must post a video or photo via. Instagram and include the hashtags #RoyalBarbell #RoyalBarbell12DaysOfFitmas and Make sure your account is public! 2. The post must include a fitness activity you have done for the day (i.e. Treadmill results, a view from your jog/bike, squat video, clean and jerk video, etc.) be creative! 3. You must make at least 12 posts following the criteria's, before December 25, 2016. 4. You must be following @RoyalBarbell on Instagram. Once you complete these requirements by the contest deadline (December 25, 2016), you will be entered in a draw for our holiday gift prize! Remember you are not required to post 12 days in a row, as long as you have made at least 12 posts by the deadline (we will be checking!) you'll automatically be entered in for the gift prize! Have fun, and stay active!
